Slide 7 ¬∑ Inside the Tangle

The Logic of the Tangle ‚Äî Cooperative Consensus

  • Each new transaction approves two previous transactions, weaving a directed graph.
  • Approved transactions gain cumulative weight and security.
  • Unapproved transactions remain as tips until selected by new arrivals.
  • Lightweight Proof-of-Work minimises spam while preserving decentralisation.
  • Swarm consensus emerges as the network continuously validates itself.

Slide 8 ¬∑ Dynamic Deposit Pricing

DLT as a Mechanism for Social Compliance

  • Users stake tokens to access resources and regain them after compliant behaviour.
  • Loss of tokens for non-compliance generates immediate accountability.
  • Micro-incentives allow IoT systems to run autonomously with economic guardrails.
  • DAGs enable high-frequency, fee-less settlement between citizens and infrastructure.
